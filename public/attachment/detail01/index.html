Hoisting hook (general purpose)

A simple hook type to match various workpiece shapes.
Ideal for hanging sling belts and special attachments, or when attachments need to be replaced.

This general-purpose model can be used for suspending various attachments.
Applications
Assists in the mounting and removal of material workpieces to and from processing machines or the transfer of completed assemblies to pallets
Max. transport weight (at 0.7 MPa)
52 kg (palletizing model)

Other attachment examples

The hook for hoisting can be used for various applications with a sub-attachment.
Sub-attachments must be provided by the customer.

    Attached to the hook, it can transport drum cans, etc.

  • Magnetic attraction

    A magnet is mounted at the tip of the hook to magnetically attract and transport metallic workpieces. Round iron parts can also be suctioned.

    View Video

  • Bag clamp

    A clamper is mounted on the hook, allowing bagged workpieces such as bags of material to be clamped and transported.

  • Hook balancer

    A hook balancer is attached on the hook to suspend and transport long workpieces.

  • Clamp for rectangular workpieces

    A clamper can be mounted on the hook to clamp and transport rectangular workpieces.
