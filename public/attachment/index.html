Attachment types

  • Hook for hoisting

    Sub-attachments (6 types) can be mounted to support various workpieces.

    Sub-attachments available!

  • For suctioning of bags

    Ideal for the suction-transport of bags weighing up to 30kg, such as bags containing materials.
    Suction tests will be performed in advance.

  • For the suction-transport of corrugated cardboards

    Ideal for the suction-transport of cardboard boxes weighing up to 30kg.
    Suction tests will be performed in advance.

  • For suctioning 18 L cans

    Ideal for the suction-transport of 18 L cans weighing up to 25kg.

  • Roll material for internal diameter clamp
    内径クランプ用

    Ideal for transporting rolled workpieces such as packaging materials.
    It is inserted into the roll core, clamped and transported.

  • Clamp for food trays

    Ideal for transporting thin containers such as food trays for food products.

*Specifications are determined according to customer requirements, so the maximum transport weight, appearance, etc., are subject to change.
