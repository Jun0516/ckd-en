FAQ

We will answer your questions.

Click here to find answers

  • Q

    What is the estimated cost of the PowerArm.

    A

    It depends on the specifications, and so please contact us using the inquiry form.
    We will contact you with a rough estimate of the cost after proposing the most suitable specifications for you.

  • Q

    Are there any specifications for the installation location?

    A

    We recommend a flat, dust-free, and waterproof location.

  • Q

    Are there any environmental requirements or preparations necessary for installation?

    A

    We ask that the customer prepare their own air source and electricity.
    For air sequencing, please prepare only the air source.
    The power supply can be used with a 100 VAC outlet, but 200 VAC may be required when using a vacuum pump.

  • Q

    Is it possible to get a tour or demonstration?

    A

    Yes, it is. Please contact us for details.

  • Q

    How long is the delivery time?

    A

    Normally, about 2 months.
    However, it depends on the specifications, so please contact us.

  • Q

    What is the maximum transfer weight?

    A

    The workpiece weight can be up to about 80 kg, but it depends on the specifications and attachments.

  • Q

    I do not know how to select the right machine.

    A

    Please fill in the inquiry form with the workpiece weight, installation location, work contents, etc., and consult with us.
    We will propose the most suitable specifications for you.

  • Q

    Is there any maintenance work needed?

    A

    We recommend that you follow the instruction manual, but please maintain and control the air quality (element). Greasing (lubrication) is not required. Our company will contact you about maintenance in the third and fifth years after purchase, so please consider it at that time.
