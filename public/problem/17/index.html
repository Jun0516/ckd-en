To Solve These Problems

PowerArm helps you lift heavy items in the following situations!

Conveyance of the packing film roll

About 90 rolls of packaging film (30 kg) are used for pharmaceutical products every day.
Before use, two people are needed to remove the packaging material, place it on a cart, and transport it.

  • Before

    Experiencing any of the following problems?

    ・There is a risk of accidentally dropping the workpiece and causing injury.

    ・Work must be done by two people.

  • After

    When the PowerArm is introduced...

    ・The dedicated attachment is flipped 90 degrees to allow safe loading onto a dolly.

    ・Packaging can now be done with one worker.
