90-degree rotation of a plate-type workpiece

This is a demonstration of the inversioning of a large plate through suctioning.The use of a special suction pad enables safe and easy inversion of workpieces without having to assume an awkward half-squatting posture.It can also be used for workpieces with large flat surfaces such as steel plates, glass, and plywood.

Suction of corrugated cardboard

Using a dedicated suction pad, the machine is useful for transporting corrugated cardboard in the receiving and shipping processes.It is especially useful in situations that are difficult for robots to handle, such as corrugated cardboard that has collapsed in the receiving process.The worker can safely and easily transfer workpieces without having to take an unstable half-squatting posture.Send us your corrugated cardboard and we will conduct a suction test.

ばんじゅう・コンテナクランプ

A dedicated clamper enables safe and easy transport of workpieces transfer without having to take an unstable half-squatting posture.
Special designs to suit different sizes are also available.

Suction of 18 L cans

Using a specially designed suction pad, safe and easy transport of workpieces is realized without having to take a half-squatting posture.It can also be used on 18 L cans with no handles.

Suction of bags

Suction is performed with a vacuum pump using the dedicated oval vacuum pad.The worker can safely and easily transfer workpieces without having to take an unstable half-squatting posture.Send us a sample of your bag and we will conduct a suction test.

一斗缶投入（液体・個体）

One unit can handle the lifting and filling of 18 L cans in the filling process of food, pharmaceutical, chemical, and other materials.An option of indicating the approximate weight during partial filling can be considered.
