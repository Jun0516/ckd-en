Subsidy Programs

Age-Friendly System

The Age Friendly Subsidy is a system that subsidizes the implementation of safety and health measures, such as improvements to the workplace environment, by small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) so that elder workers can work safely and with peace of mind.

Where to apply
Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare
Application Deadline
May 11 - October 31, 2022
Subsidy Amount
Max. 1 million yen
Subsidy rate
One-half
Eligibility
(1) Employs at least one elderly worker (age 60 or older) on a regular basis
(2) Small and medium-sized enterprises in the retail, service, wholesale, and other industries (Detailed regulations exist)
(3) The company is covered by labor insurance.
* In addition, there is a screening process before a decision is made.

Please refer to the applicant's website for details.

Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare　Details of Age-Friendly Subsidy

The information provided is for the fiscal year 2022. Please check the above official website for the latest information.

Subsidy for Promotion of Work-style Reform

Subsidies are available for small and medium-sized businesses that work to improve productivity, reduce overtime work, and create an environment that promotes annual paid leave and special leave.

Where to apply
Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare
Application Deadline
May 11 - October 31, 2022
Subsidy Amount
Max. 1.5 million yen
Subsidy rate
Four-fifths (varies with conditions)
Eligibility
Small and medium-sized business owners who meet certain conditions

Please refer to the applicant's website for details.

Detailed page of the Subsidy for Promotion and Support of Work-style Reform

The information provided is for the fiscal year 2022. Please check the above official website for the latest information.

Manufacturing Subsidy

Subsidies for the promotion of manufacturing, commerce, and service productivity support the development of innovative services, prototype development, and production processes undertaken by SMEs and small-scale enterprises, etc., in order to cope with the system changes that SMEs and small-scale enterprises will face in succession over the next several years, such as work-style reforms, the application of employee insurance, wage increases, introduction of invoices, etc.

Where to apply
National Federation of Small Business Associations
Application Deadline
Up to November 30, 2022
Subsidy Amount
Max. 30 million yen
Subsidy rate
Upper limit of 2/3
Eligibility
For small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) making capital investment for management innovation, etc.

Please refer to the applicant's website for details.
